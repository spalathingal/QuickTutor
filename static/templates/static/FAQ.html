Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register?

When you log in the first time to QuickTutor through Google, your account is registered. If you want to change anything on your profile, click on the Update Profile link on the Profile page.

How can I become a tutor?

Anyone can be a tutor! There is no registration process. To become a live tutor, all you have to do is update your profile to show all of the subjects you can help out with! After this, make sure to "go live" by ensuring that your Active Tutor box is checked.

How do tutors get paid?

All payments will be processed by our system online. We will accept payments made through Stripe.

How can I get help from a tutor?

On the home page, go to the "See list of tutors" link to see all of the active tutors. You can search through the list to find a tutor who fits your needs. Then, after clicking on their name, you can send them a message to ask clarifying questions or send them a request to be tutored! If your request for help is accepted, you will be notified on the "List of Tutors" page, and if your request is denied, it will be removed from your pending requests.

How much does tutoring cost?

Pricing for QuickTutor is fixed depending on the length of time you requested. When you fill out a request to be tutored, you must select a time interval for how long you would like to be tutored for. Each time interval has a set amount that your tutor will be paid, regardless of how long the actual meeting was. (For example, if you request a tutor for 15-30 minutes, you will have to pay X amount, no matter how long your session lasts.)

Is it safe?

Yes. We do not ask for private information, and meeting places are restricted to pre-approved, public university study areas.
